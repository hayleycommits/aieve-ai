The problem

Your leads are leaking out five different ways.

Every home-service business loses jobs the same way — calls that go unanswered, forms nobody reads till Monday, and homeowners who already booked your competitor while you were on a roof.

Missed calls

1 in 3 enquiries go to voicemail. 80% don't ring back. They ring the next firm down the list.

After-hours forms

Homeowners fill the form at 9pm. By morning they've already booked someone who replied at 9:01.

Slow callbacks

Response over 5 minutes drops conversion by 80%. Your office can't keep up with the inbox.

Price shoppers

Endless "how much for a new roof?" calls eat the day with leads that never had budget.

Unqualified enquiries

Wrong area, wrong service, wrong scope — no filter at the top of the funnel, so your crew wastes site visits.

The fix

AI EVE plugs every leak.

One AI agent lives on your website and your phone line. She picks up in under a second, holds a real conversation, filters tyre-kickers and drops booked jobs straight into your calendar.

// 01

Answers instantly

Picks up in 0.4s, day or night. Voice or chat. Never a voicemail again.

// 02

Qualifies the lead

Asks the trade-specific questions a senior estimator would. Filters scope, area, urgency.

// 03

Books the call

Checks your crews' availability and locks the slot live — homeowner gets a confirmation on the spot.

// 04

Sends follow-up

SMS + email reminders before the visit. Reschedules quietly if a job overruns.

// 05

Updates your calendar

Two-way sync with Google, Outlook & ServiceTitan. CRM gets a fully-tagged contact and call transcript.
