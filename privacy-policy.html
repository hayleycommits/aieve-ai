Privacy Policy

How AI EVE handles website and enquiry data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through aieve.ai, how we use it, and how to contact us if you have questions or want us to update or delete your information.

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Who we are

AI EVE provides AI voice and enquiry-handling services for independent hotels and hospitality businesses. If you have questions about this policy or want to make a privacy request, you can contact us at hello@aieve.ai.

What we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Your name, company, email address, phone number, website, and message content when you submit an enquiry form.
  • Booking information you choose to provide when you schedule a call through our calendar link.
  • Audio, microphone access, and call interaction data if you choose to use the live voice demo on the website.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, device information, and server or request logs created when you visit the site.

How we use information

We use information collected through the site to:

  • Respond to enquiries and booking requests.
  • Arrange and deliver demos, callbacks, or follow-up conversations.
  • Understand how prospects want to use AI EVE and tailor our response.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the website and our lead-handling process.

Third-party services used on this site

Some actions on the site are provided by third-party services rather than being handled entirely on our own servers.

  • Web3Forms is used to process website enquiry form submissions.
  • Cal.com is used to display and manage call booking availability.
  • Vapi is used to power the live voice demo and microphone-based interaction.
  • Google Fonts is used to load the fonts displayed on the site.

When you use these parts of the site, your information may be processed by those providers under their own terms and privacy policies as well as ours.

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry and booking information for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, manage our relationship with you, and maintain basic business records. If you ask us to delete your information, we will review that request and remove data where we are not required to keep it.

How we share information

We do not sell personal information collected through this site. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, manage enquiries, run demos, or schedule calls. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights and systems.

Your choices

You can choose not to use the enquiry form, booking form, or live voice demo. You can also contact us to:

  • request a copy of the information we hold about you,
  • ask us to correct inaccurate information, or
  • ask us to delete information where we no longer need it.

To make a request, email hello@aieve.ai.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.
